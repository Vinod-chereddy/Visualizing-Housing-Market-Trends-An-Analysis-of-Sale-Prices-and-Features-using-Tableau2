Visualizing Housing Market Trends: An Analysis of Sale Prices and Features using Tableau

  • The goal is to address challenges in understanding the factors that influence house prices and sales trends.
  • By analyzing comprehensive housing data including years since renovation, room counts, and house age, the goal is to uncover key insights.
  • Utilizing Tableau, the objective is to visualize and interpret patterns to enhance market competitiveness.
